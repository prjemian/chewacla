property vocabulary: dict#
vector(symbol: str) → numpy.ndarray#

Convert a symbol like ‘x+’ or ‘+x’ to a numpy array (copy).

Behavior: - Accepts ‘x+’ (letter then sign) or ‘+x’ (sign then letter). - Does NOT permute characters; validates positions instead. - Whitespace allowed; case-insensitive.
